The Collection
How is McDonald's so successful across the world? It is more than their quality fries and great deals; it is the company's ability to successfully integrate into unique cultures. McDonald's has teams of people dedicated to market research that determine exactly what it is people of unique cultures are drawn to. This includes everything from interior design to new product introduction.
In this collection we will explore the similarities and differences in exterior, interior, product offerings, and advertisements of McDonald's across the globe. This photo collection is only a peek into the successful cultural integration of the fast-food giant, but it is a start.
